Baby on board! There’s no doubt that having a baby dramatically shifts your eating habits. From eating for two whilst pregnant then having a lot less time and a whole new routine makes preparing regular dinners a daily struggle. Lack of time is the number one obstacle to balanced eating habits during pregnancy and growing your family. Top tip from us – plan as much as possible and outsource. When it comes to cooking, we know kids love anything crumbed, so get ready to get your hands dirty!

Fleeing the nest! More often than not, moving away from the family home is the first big step towards your own independence, so it’s no surprise your eating habits change as well. We get it – young people have a lot on their plate between study, work and socialising! For parents, you should know that at least half of young Aussies say they cook less than two times a week after moving out and 2 in 5 gain unwanted weight.
